HELP OUT PEWDIEPIE IN HIS BATTLE AGAINST T-SERIES BY SUBSCRIBING TO HIM

Who are we?

This is a very short project for a test run of the longer IGCSE project in Year 11. So I am using this time and chance to promote Pewdiepie, who is in desperate need of help.(This website was put together in about three hours so it's not perfect)

What is our purpose?

This website is to make merchandise promote Pewdiepie to our fellow internet users oblivious or neutral on the Pewdiepie vs T-Series issue to subscribe to Pewdiepie and unsubscribe from T-Series. It is needed because Pewdiepie is a symbol/icon of YouTube's individual content creators, which was its original audience when YouTube was created. Another reason why is to raise awareness against the act of sub-botting and corporate control on YouTube.

How this website works

This website has two pages: a home page (this one) and a products page where I introduce all of my products (10 in total). You can choose between the two pages by clicking on the navigation buttons in the header.
